# LeoNes — Hero Launch: The Sparkle Pride

Five SKUs, one color story (Pastel Sky → Baby Pink gradient), one shared magical mechanic per product, priced as gift-worthy premium — not toy-aisle, not luxury-adult. Target price band: $12–$28, sweet spot $16.

## 1. Cloud Gloss — $14
**What it is:** Clear lip gloss that warms to a soft pink the moment it hits your lips.
**Magic:** Thermochromic pigment — colorless in the tube, blooms baby pink from body heat within seconds.
**Packaging:** Frosted dome tube, cloud-shaped cap, holographic base that catches light.
**Safety proof point:** Dermatologist-tested, fragrance from natural sources only, ages 8+.
**Photo hero:** Gloss suspended mid-air over crystal water, droplet catching light.

## 2. Star Oil — $16
**What it is:** Nourishing lip oil with genuinely suspended edible-glitter "stars" that swirl when shaken.
**Magic:** Visible suspended shimmer particles — the "shake it and watch" moment, built for video.
**Packaging:** Round glass-look bottle (shatter-safe acrylic), star-shaped wand cap.
**Safety proof point:** Cosmetic-grade mica only, no plastic microglitter — call this out explicitly (parents search for this).
**Photo hero:** Macro shot mid-shake, stars caught in motion blur.

## 3. Mane Mist — $18
**What it is:** A fine hair mist — light shimmer + light hold, smells like sun-warmed vanilla and berries.
**Magic:** Fine gold shimmer visible only in sunlight/flash — invisible indoors, "roars" in photos and outside.
**Packaging:** Tall pastel sky bottle, gold cap shaped like a subtle mane-texture ridge.
**Safety proof point:** Alcohol-light formula, tear-free spray zone, ages 8+.
**Photo hero:** Backlit spray burst, shimmer catching sun flare.

## 4. Confetti Cheek Tint — $16
**What it is:** Cream-to-powder cheek and lip tint, blush-ball applicator.
**Magic:** Color-adapts slightly to skin pH — "your own shade," not one-size-fits-all, framed as *personal*, not corrective.
**Packaging:** Compact pebble shape, fits in a small palm, mirror inside lid.
**Safety proof point:** Talc-free, non-comedogenic, patch-test callout on pack.
**Photo hero:** Fingertip dab shot on the cheek, soft-focus bloom of color.

## 5. Pride Pouch — $12 (add-on / gift-with-purchase threshold unlock)
**What it is:** Quilted holographic pouch with a collectible enamel lioness charm zipper pull.
**Magic:** Charm is part of a collectible series (this launch = "First Roar" gold charm); different charm per seasonal Pride — drives repeat purchase.
**Packaging:** The product *is* the packaging — no separate box, ships in reusable branded mailer.
**Photo hero:** Flat-lay of full Pride collection nested inside the open pouch.

## Bundling & funnel logic
- **"Starter Pride" bundle** ($54, vs $64 à la carte): Cloud Gloss + Star Oil + Pride Pouch — the impulse-gift SKU for parents.
- **Full Pride** ($76, vs $90 à la carte): all five — the collector/completionist SKU, marketed to the girl directly via social.
- Every order ships with 1 random collectible sticker sheet (low-cost, high perceived value, drives unboxing content).

## Why these five, in this order
Gloss and oil are the lowest-trust-barrier, lowest-price entry points (parents already buy lip balm) — they carry the brand's two signature magic mechanics (thermochromic + suspended shimmer) that are most video-native. Mane Mist and Cheek Tint expand the ritual once trust is established. The Pouch turns single-purchase customers into collectors and funds the loyalty mechanic ("roars," see brand strategy doc) without needing a sixth formula SKU for launch.
