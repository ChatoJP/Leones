# LeoNes Launch Content Library

Platform-organized, launch-ready. Brand spelling is exactly **LeoNes** everywhere.
All video ads in `tiktok/ads/branded-*` carry the real logo overlaid top-center —
always use the `branded-` versions publicly (AI-generated on-product text drifts).

## Structure
- `tiktok/ads/` — 11 brand-locked concept ads (24s, audio) + chase/plush/sting clips
- `tiktok/loops/` — 10 product loops + collection film + new macro loops (8s, seamless)
- `tiktok/covers/` + `instagram/reels-covers/` — poster frames + designed covers
- `instagram/feed/` — square posts (drop, quote, besties, unboxing)
- `instagram/feed-square-video/` — 1:1 center-crops of hero loops
- `instagram/carousels/` — carousel-how (5), carousel-plush (4), carousel-unbox (4)
- `instagram/stories/` — vertical story visuals
- `facebook/ads/` — wide ad creatives (product / plush / unboxing angles ×2 each)
- `website/heroes/` + `website/banners/` — hero backgrounds, section banners
- `brand/logos/` — wordmark (plum + light), app icon, generated variants
- `brand/mascots/` — Boop/Cloudie/Jelly cutouts, poses, group, chase scenes
- `brand/stickers/` — die-cut stickers + kawaii icons
- `brand/overlays/` — glitter/bokeh/hearts on black (blend mode: screen)
- `brand/templates/` — empty reusable frames (stories, posts, banners)
- `products/` — per-SKU renders, scenes, swatches, packaging, bag/gift shots
- `campaigns/` — seasonal + occasion creatives
- `thumbnails/` — wide video thumbnails (space right for title)

## Usage rules
- Never let "LeoNes" be respelled; overlay text belongs in the edit, not the asset.
- CTA cards ("coming soon", "limited drop"…) are EN masters — localize in the editor.
- No fake ratings/testimonials exist here by design; keep it that way.
- PNG = master quality (ads/print). WEBP = web. Videos are 9:16 MP4 unless in feed-square-video.
